### Out at Sea
I spent much of the second half of 2015 on an oil rig in the southern North Sea. There was no land in sight. 

Out at Sea is a dumb web experiment but also possibly a way for me to step back into the specific in-between moment I found myself in 11 years ago. It is a quiet digital space for pausing and letting your mind drift.

#### Interaction
* Look around: Click and drag (or swipe) left and right to look around the full 360-degree horizon. 
* Change the time of day: Drag up and down to move the sun.

#### Built with:
* Three.js (WebGL): Handles the 3D space, camera math and animations.
* Water and Sky Shaders: Built-in extensions for Three.js.
* Web Audio API: Background wind sound is made from random noise with digital filters.
